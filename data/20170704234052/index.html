      About this Add-on

      Diigo is the #1 online service for annotating, bookmarking, archiving and sharing webpages, plus more! This official extension by Diigo is a full-featured toolbar for power users. If you like a light-weight solution, you may prefer Diigolet instead.

      With Diigo Toolbar, you can

      1. Save links or the whole webpages online
      2. Attach highlights & stickies to a webpage as a reminder
      3. Share pages with annotation via Twitter, Facebook, Google Buzz
      4. Take screenshot of a whole page or any portion, and annotate
      5. Save everything online for access anywhere, via free apps on iPhone, Android , and iPad .
      6. Create groups to pool findings, share resources or curate content
