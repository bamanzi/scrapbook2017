              Privacy Policy

              If syncing is turned on, your reading list will be sent to Read It Later servers.

              The data on the server is used for these purposes:
              1. To provide the user with a RSS feed and remote access to their reading list.
              2. To allow syncing of their list between the user's computers.
              3. General usage statistics and anonymous trending for Read It Later

              Please view the Read It Later service's privacy policy at : http://readitlaterlist.com/privacy
